About us

Our Foundation:

Sea Advisers provides tailored transportation services to its clients for more than 10 years. These services include project planning, route surveys, assistance with lift engineering, project management and cargo shipment.

Our Passion:

Even if we are small sized biz , we - as a solution partner, plan every stage to the smallest detail prior to the beginning of your project upon completion. Transport services are conducted by the use of international standards, state of the art equipment and under the supervision on -site, if needed.

As a logistics service provider, we are constantly looking for optimal solutions for our customers in a changing world. We do this by using information and communication technology, based on integrity and transparency.

Our Motivation:

We offer our services to a broad range of sectors. Each industry has its individual working standards and safety requirements. We make sure that you receive the support you need and the transport will be executed in a timely and safe manner.

Our strength:

If you want to ship more weight with a single transport, or to help us reduce the environmental impact of our activities, choose our proposed intermodal solutions. For our intermodal projects we use an integrated network in Europe which is able to guarantee a most competitive service. On that note, if you want to ship heavy weights, we would be more than delighted to assist!





Our know how

  • Also we can handle shipments via Danube up North the continent.
  • We can transport any kind of cargo by route.
  • We can provide logistic services and auxiliary services connected to transport.
