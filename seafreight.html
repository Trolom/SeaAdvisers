Sea Freight

We can arrange sea/ocean transportation to/from Constanta/Agigea ports to all main ports world wide.

We have the know how to plan river transports on the Danube to/from Romanian ports. We can reach either Regensburg or further North Continent ports.

Besides river/ocean freight , we can provide following additional services concerning cargo movement:

  • We have expertise in moving project cargo from door to door ;
  • We also have connections with main shipping lines for containers transport;

Over the years we have established solid partnerships with the leading ocean carriers which allows us to offer worldwide coverage.

Our solutions include:

  • Full Container Load (FCL) shipments
  • Consolidated LCL (Less Container Load) shipments
  • Breakbulk, Heavy Lifts and Ro-Ro shipments
  • Part and full vessel charter services
  • Management of import/export customs clearance
