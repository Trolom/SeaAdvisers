Road Freight

Special transport:

We can arrange transport for long, high and wide loads by road using the best carriers in Romania and Europe. We transport freight such as construction machinery, agricultural equipment, silos, cable reels, industrial equipment, etc. Every transport is carried out and handled in a safe and efficient manner, without any effort required on your part.

Heavy transport:

We have connections with specialized transporters who use modular equipment, so that we can build the right trailers for your transport. This means that even abnormal loads that are too large for a standard equipment can be transported. In addition to any 4- and 8-axle modular basic equipment, we can provide drum bridges for very high and heavy loads and various customised parts for other specialist sectors, like windmills or oil extraction gear.

FTL services:

Competent staff at your service : we work on a dynamic and consistent order portfolio, ensuring a competitive advantage in terms of flexibility and performance. For road and sea routes, we work with industry-leading partners, to whom we are bound by long-term agreements and mutual commitment on volumes and service. This is how we maintain a competitive position in an increasingly demanding market.
